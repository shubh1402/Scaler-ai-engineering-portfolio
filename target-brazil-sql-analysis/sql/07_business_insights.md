# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Insights

### 1. Growing E-commerce Adoption
Analysis showed a steady increase in order volumes over the years, indicating rapid growth of e-commerce adoption in the Brazilian market.

### 2. Monthly Order Seasonality
Monthly trend analysis revealed seasonal spikes in customer orders during specific months, highlighting the impact of shopping trends and promotional periods.

### 3. Customer Activity by Time of Day
Most Brazilian customers placed orders during afternoon and night hours, suggesting peak shopping activity outside standard working hours.

### 4. Delivery Performance Variations
Certain states experienced significantly higher average delivery times and freight charges, indicating logistics inefficiencies and regional operational challenges.

### 5. Payment Installment Preference
A large number of customers preferred installment-based payments, reflecting customer demand for flexible payment options in online purchasing.

### 6. Freight Cost Impact
States with longer delivery distances generally exhibited higher freight costs, directly impacting operational expenses and delivery efficiency.

---

# Business Recommendations

## 1. Optimize Delivery Operations
States with consistently high delivery times should be analyzed for logistics bottlenecks. Regional distribution centers and local delivery partnerships can improve operational efficiency.

## 2. Improve Customer Experience
Implement delivery tracking enhancements and post-delivery feedback systems to improve transparency and customer satisfaction.

## 3. Expand Flexible Payment Options
Since installment-based payments are highly utilized, Target can introduce customized financing offers and promotional EMI plans to improve conversion rates.

## 4. Regional Marketing Strategies
Customer ordering patterns vary significantly across states. Localized marketing campaigns can improve engagement and regional sales performance.

## 5. Freight Cost Optimization
Analyze high-freight regions and optimize warehouse allocation and shipping routes to reduce operational delivery costs.

# Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL-driven analytics can be used to extract operational, financial, and customer insights from large-scale e-commerce datasets. The analysis highlights opportunities for improving logistics efficiency, customer experience, and payment strategy optimization for Target Brazil.
