# Jamboree Admission Prediction

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the factors influencing graduate school admission chances using historical applicant data from Jamboree Education.

The objective is to identify the most important predictors of admission success and build a machine learning model capable of estimating admission probability.

---

# Business Problem

Students applying for higher education programs often struggle to understand how different academic and profile factors affect admission decisions.

This analysis aims to:

* Understand key admission drivers
* Analyze applicant characteristics
* Evaluate feature importance
* Predict admission probability
* Provide data-driven recommendations for applicants

# Business Insights

* CGPA is one of the strongest indicators of admission success.
* GRE and TOEFL scores significantly influence admission probability.
* Academic profile consistency improves admission chances.
* Predictive modeling can help applicants estimate admission likelihood before applying.
